FAQ'S

How do I clean and care for my jewellery?

Most jewellery except corals and pearls (since they are organic) are cleaned by dipping them into a bath of lukewarm baby shampoo water,
and cleaned with a soft-bristled toothbrush. The jewellery is then wiped dry with a soft towel which does not tug at the metal settings of the gemstones.

Can I see a sample of the jewellery piece ordered for before final completion?

Yes. In fact, we recommend a sighting of the jewellery piece at the “ghat” stage.

What services do you offer?

The services offered at Gehna are as follows
1.Customised Jewellery design
2.Customised Jewellery manufacturing
3.In-house jewellery design and manufacturing
4.Jewellery evaluation
5.Jewellery cleaning and polishing
6.Buying and selling of gold and silver bars or coins.

Do you provide any guarantees for your jewellery?

Gehna prides itself in the fact that all aspects of making the jewellery are personally looked at the Mr.Sunith Samdaria,
a person whose experience pans for more than 20 years in the diamond business, right from sourcing of diamonds and gemstones, to the supervision of design and to the last detail in the manufacturing of the pieces.
Hence, all jewellery has his personal assurance of the highest quality. Besides this, Gehna does also provide a guarantee in writing of the jewellery that goes out of Gehna.

What would be the gestation period to execute a client’s jewellery requirement? Is there a long wait for custom orders?

No, the wait is not long by any standards. We take an average of 2-4 weeks for open and closed setting south Indian styles of jewellery making,
and an average of 8-16 weeks for the Kundan style based on the scale of the piece.
